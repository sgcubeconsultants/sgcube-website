Section 01

About This Agreement

This Beta Participation Agreement and Privacy Policy ("Agreement") governs your participation in the closed beta testing programme for UDAY ("the App"), a discipline tracking application currently under development. The App is owned and operated by Sayantan Ghosh, operating through SGCube Consultants ("we," "us," "the Founder").

By tapping "I Agree and Continue," you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms of this Agreement.

Section 02

Nature of Beta Participation

2.1 You are participating in this beta programme as a voluntary tester. Your participation does not constitute any form of employment, freelance engagement, contractual service, or commercial relationship with the Founder or SGCube Consultants.

2.2 Your participation carries no expectation of, and you hereby waive any claim to, monetary compensation, equity, revenue share, profit participation, royalties, or any other financial interest in the App, its parent product, or any future commercial proceeds arising from the App or any related product.

2.3 Any feedback, suggestions, bug reports, ideas, or improvements you provide during the beta programme may be freely used by the Founder to develop, improve, or modify the App, without any obligation to compensate you, credit you, or seek further consent.

2.4 You acknowledge that the App is in an early development stage. It may contain bugs, errors, or incomplete features. You agree to use it at your own risk.

Section 03

Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

3.1 The App, its features, design, architecture, user interface, and functionality are confidential and proprietary. You agree not to disclose, describe, reproduce, or share any aspect of the App — including screenshots, screen recordings, or verbal descriptions of unreleased features — with any third party without prior written permission from the Founder.

3.2 This confidentiality obligation remains in effect for 24 months from the date of your beta access, or until the App is publicly launched, whichever is earlier.

3.3 You agree not to reverse engineer, decompile, copy, replicate, or attempt to extract the App's source code, logic, algorithms, or design.

Section 04

Data We Collect

During your participation in the beta programme, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Your unique beta access code
  • In-app usage data (pillar scores, daily logs, FIRE calculator inputs, streaks, and session data)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Timestamp of activity

You confirm that all personal data you submit is your own and not data belonging to any third party.

Section 05

How We Use Your Data

5.1 Your data is collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To identify you as a verified beta tester
  • To improve the App's features, performance, and user experience
  • To communicate with you about the beta programme
  • To understand how users interact with the App

5.2 Your data will not be used for advertising, marketing to third parties, or sold or licensed to any external entity.

5.3 Aggregate, anonymised insights derived from beta usage may be used by the Founder for product decision-making.

Section 06

Data Storage and Security

6.1 Your personal data is stored on MongoDB Atlas (AWS Mumbai region, ap-south-1) and is accessible only to the Founder.

6.2 Reasonable technical and organisational security measures are in place to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6.3 Your data will be retained for the duration of the beta programme and deleted within 90 days of the App's public launch, unless you request earlier deletion.

Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact: sgcubeconsultants@gmail.com
