About SGCube

A boutique consultancy built on practice, not theory.

SGCube Consultants was born from a simple observation: the most valuable commercial opportunities sit at the intersection of disciplines — where AdTech meets storytelling, where data meets culture, where strategy meets execution. SGCube is the culmination of 11+ years of hands-on experience across India's most competitive digital and content environments — a firm built by someone who has sat on every side of the table.

Our work is grounded in real platform experience — from scaling programmatic revenue at Bobble AI, to building branded content partnerships at Condé Nast, to growing creator monetisation at ShareChat and Gaana. We bring that accumulated perspective to every engagement, regardless of where you are in your journey.

We believe good consulting is indistinguishable from good partnership. We don't parachute in with frameworks and leave. We stay close to the problem, work alongside your team and measure our success by the outcomes we help create — not the decks we deliver.
